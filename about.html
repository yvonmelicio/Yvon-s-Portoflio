YVE

Yve or Yvon T. Melicio is a 20 year old, 3rd Year AB Multimedia Arts student from Lyceum of the Philippines University. Yve is a digital artist, a graphic designer, a writer, and a game designer and developer.

Yve aims to share stories with their art and their variety of art styles. Yve also aspires to create a bigger and much more entertaining game with beautiful stories than the two that they have already made.

Yve is an independent artist who can work on small and large scale projects. They are also proficient in not just drawing and designing but also in programming.
